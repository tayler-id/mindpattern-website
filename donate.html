Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

+

Yes, donations to MindPattern are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt for your donation.

How is my donation used?

+

Your donation directly supports our mission to make mental health pattern recognition accessible to everyone. Funds are used to provide free access to those in need, develop new features, conduct research, and expand our reach to underserved communities.

Can I make a donation in honor of someone?

+

Yes, you can make a donation in honor or memory of someone. During the donation process, you'll have the option to dedicate your gift and send a notification to the honoree or their family.

How do I cancel my monthly donation?

+

You can cancel your monthly donation at any time by logging into your donor account or contacting our support team at donations@mindpattern.com. Your cancellation will take effect immediately, and you won't be charged for future months.

Can I donate if I'm outside the United States?

+

Yes, we accept donations from around the world. International donors can use credit cards, PayPal, or wire transfers. Please note that tax benefits may vary depending on your country's regulations.

How can I get a receipt for my donation?

+

You'll automatically receive a receipt via email after your donation is processed. If you need another copy or have questions about your receipt, please contact us at donations@mindpattern.com.
