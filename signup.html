Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about signing up.

How does the free trial work?

+

All plans come with a 14-day free trial. You'll need to enter your payment information to start, but you won't be charged until the trial ends. You can cancel anytime during the trial period.

Can I change my plan later?

+

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes to your plan will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How do I cancel my subscription?

+

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your plan until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your personal information and mental health data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your individual data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

+

Yes, you can access your account on any device by logging in with your email and password. Your data will sync automatically across all your devices.

Do you offer discounts for students or healthcare workers?

+

Yes, we offer special pricing for students, educators, healthcare workers, and non-profit organizations. Please contact our support team with verification of your status to learn more.
