Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How quickly can I expect a response?

+

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

Do you offer technical support on weekends?

+

Yes, our technical support team is available on weekends from 10am to 4pm EST for urgent issues. Regular inquiries will be addressed on the next business day.

Can I schedule a demo of the app?

+

Absolutely! You can schedule a personalized demo by selecting "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form subject line and mentioning your interest in a demo.

How can I provide feedback about the app?

+

We value your feedback! You can use the contact form above and select "Feedback" as the subject, or email us directly at feedback@mindpattern.com.
