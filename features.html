How Our Technology Works

A glimpse into the advanced technology behind our pattern recognition system.

1. Data Collection

Minimal data points are collected through quick daily check-ins and optional passive monitoring (with your permission).

2. Pattern Analysis

Our proprietary machine learning algorithms analyze your data to identify correlations and patterns that might affect your mental health.

3. Contextual Understanding

The system considers environmental factors, time patterns, and behavioral contexts to provide a holistic view of your mental health.

4. Personalized Insights
