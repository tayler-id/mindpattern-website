Business Model & Revenue Streams

How we generate sustainable revenue and growth.

Subscription Model

Our core revenue comes from tiered subscription plans (Basic, Premium, Ultimate) with monthly and annual billing options.

  • Basic: $4.99/month or $47.88/year (20% savings)
  • Premium: $9.99/month or $95.88/year (20% savings)
  • Ultimate: $14.99/month or $143.88/year (20% savings)

Enterprise Solutions

Custom implementations for healthcare providers, employers, and educational institutions with volume-based pricing.

  • Healthcare providers: Integration with existing patient management systems
  • Employers: Employee wellness programs with aggregated analytics
  • Educational institutions: Student mental health support systems

API Licensing

  • Usage-based pricing model

Investor FAQ

Answers to common questions about investing in our company.

What is the minimum investment amount?

+

Our minimum investment amount is $10,000 for the Seed Investor tier. However, we're open to discussing different arrangements for strategic partners.

What type of equity is being offered?

+

We're offering preferred equity shares with pro-rata rights for future funding rounds. Specific terms are outlined in our investment agreement, which we'll share during discussions.

What is your current valuation?

+

Our current pre-money valuation is $8 million, based on our technology, market opportunity, early traction, and team expertise. Detailed valuation information is available in our pitch deck.

What is your exit strategy?

+

We're building for long-term growth, with potential exit opportunities including acquisition by larger health tech companies or mental health providers, or an IPO within 5-7 years. Our focus is on building sustainable value.

How will the investment funds be used?

+

Investment funds will be allocated approximately as follows: 40% for product development and AI enhancement, 30% for marketing and user acquisition, 20% for team expansion, and 10% for operational expenses.

What investor reporting can I expect?

+

Depending on your investment tier, you'll receive monthly or quarterly investor updates covering key metrics, financial performance, product development, and strategic initiatives. All investors are invited to our annual investor meeting.
